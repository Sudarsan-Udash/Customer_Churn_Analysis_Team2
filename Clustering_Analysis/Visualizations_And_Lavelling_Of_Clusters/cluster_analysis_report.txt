CLUSTER VISUALISATION & ANALYSIS


Number of Clusters: 3



Cluster 0 — Loyal High-Rollers
- High tenure
- High monthly charges
- Low churn risk

Cluster 1 — At-Risk Newbies
- Low tenure
- Moderate monthly charges
- High churn risk

Cluster 2 — Stable Budgeteers
- Moderate tenure
- Lower monthly charges
- Moderate churn risk



VISUALISATION
A scatter plot was created using tenure and monthly charges,
where customers are grouped into three distinct clusters.

Each cluster is colour-coded, clearly showing separation
between customer segments based on usage and spending.



INSIGHTS
- Cluster 1 (At-Risk Newbies) represents customers with the
  highest churn risk and requires targeted retention strategies.

- Cluster 0 (Loyal High-Rollers) represents high-value customers
  who contribute significant revenue and should be retained
  through loyalty programs.

- Cluster 2 (Stable Budgeteers) represents stable customers
  who can be targeted for upselling and cross-selling.


CONCLUSION
The clustering approach successfully segments customers into
meaningful groups, enabling data-driven decision-making for
customer retention, marketing, and revenue optimization.
